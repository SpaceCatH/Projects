What's the deal with Shuffleboard?

Shuffle N' Flow started with a group of friends just looking for a fun new activity. Coincidentally, the Royal Palms Shuffleboard Club had just opened its Chicago location in May 2018. Lured by the tropical decor, refreshing cocktails, and party-minded staff, the friends decided to give shuffleboard a shot. They were instantly hooked! This sport is a phyiscal chess match, relying on a combination of skill and strategy. Players are outwardly friendly and cordial but inwardly out for blood! Sounds fun, right?
