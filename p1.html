The Random Benefits Enrollment Project

One of the first projects I worked on was building a simple Benefits Enrollment experience within Google Sheets. Why did I do it?

  1. I wanted to give myself a ben-admin-related challange.
  2. I wanted to explore the idea of simplifying the enrollment experience.
  3. I didn't want to pay for Microsoft Excel. Google Sheets is free!
  4. I wanted to stretch my creative legs.

Now, this of course doesn't replace more powerful ben admin platforms, but think about how interesting it is that this could be built entirely on a FREE platform. Small businesses need a quick/easy mechanism for recording enrollments? Easy. Other industries needing basic calculations with dependencies and clean interface? No problem!

Looking back, this works better when transferred into a web-based experience, but I hadn't learned that at the time. Now that I have taken Meta courses in front-end development, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this is absolutely a possibility.
