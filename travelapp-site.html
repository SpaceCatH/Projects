TravelApp

TravelApp is a mobile tool that helps users build and share personalized trip itineraries.

Email Use

When a user creates a trip itinerary in the app, they can choose to email a copy of that itinerary to themselves or a few contacts they specify. The app converts the trip information into a clean, user-friendly HTML email using a predefined system template.

All emails are user-initiated and transactional. Users cannot send bulk or marketing messages, and there are no mailing lists, subscriptions, or promotional content.

Privacy and Safety

The app only sends emails that the user explicitly requests. We use Amazon Simple Email Service (SES) to handle reliable delivery and automatically process bounces and complaints via Amazon SNS.
