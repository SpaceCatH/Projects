Stamp’d

Stamp’d is a mobile app that helps travelers plan, organize, and share packing lists and trip details with ease.

Email Use

Stamp’d allows users to email their own trip summaries and packing lists to themselves or a few chosen contacts. Each email is generated automatically from the trip data they’ve created in the app and sent only when the user requests it.

All emails are user-initiated and transactional — never promotional or bulk messages. The app does not send newsletters, marketing emails, or unsolicited content.

Privacy and Delivery

Stamp’d uses Amazon Simple Email Service (SES) to handle secure and reliable delivery. Bounce and complaint notifications are managed through Amazon SNS to maintain high email quality.

The app does not store or share recipient information beyond what’s required to send the requested email.

Developer

Stamp’d is developed by Jonathan Eng, an independent software developer focused on creating simple, user-centered travel tools.
